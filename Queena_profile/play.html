Fish of Fortune

Fish of Fortune is a playful, voice-controlled web game that transforms simple sound into an expressive underwater experience. Instead of using traditional controls, players guide a fishing hook through the ocean using their voice, like humming or whistling to move the hook vertically while steering horizontally with the mouse. Lower tones send the hook deeper into the sea, while higher pitches lift it toward the surface, creating a fluid and intuitive interaction between sound and motion.

Paws & Progress

Paws & Progress is an interactive web application that transforms traditional pet adoption profiles into a playful, story-driven experience. Instead of relying on static text descriptions, the project allows users to explore their compatibility with a foster animal through a dynamic matching system and a short interactive care simulation. Users begin by adjusting lifestyle preferences, which continuously updates a matched pet profile in real time. Once a match is confirmed, they enter a six-stage interaction where each decision shapes the pet's emotional journey, visualized through a "Mood Map."
